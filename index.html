About Us

In order to truly experience Pakistani Food and all it has to offer, take a moment to get to know its founder, the Aqsa Kellerhoff. Aqsa, is a a TCK of Pakistani Origin, came to Morocco in Aug 2015 as a diplomat family.like any other pakistani, she is found of spices and masala food. What she noticed like many other internationals that no where in Rabat there is pakistani/indian resturant or take away. while she was working as a teacher in Rabat American School, her friends and colleagues convinced her to start a take away and give it a try, thats how the idea of Pakistani-Dhaba was born. initilly she started as only take away, Freshly Home Made pre oderd food,which she prepared herself, but shortly it became very famouse and went viral. suddenly Pakistani-Dhaba was talk f the town and started getting bigger orders for cattering and all. Observing the demand, with the help of and indian friend Aqsa decided to open a restaurant in main town of Rabat.Pakistani-Dhaba offer the real taste of Pakistan and india. Come be a part of it.
